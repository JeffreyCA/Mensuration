Instructions

This app have two modes, 2D and 3D; In your selected option, just choose the your shape and follow the commands.

In 2D mode; Square, rectangle, triangle, rhombus, parallelogram and circle are available; You have to enter the asked dimensions of the shape and this app will give you the perimeter and area of that object.

In 3D mode; Cube, cuboid, cone, cylinder, sphere and hemisphere are available; You have to enter the asked dimensions of the shape and this app will give you the surface areas, area and volume of that object.

Note:- This app takes the value of pi upto six decimal places.
So the result will be shown in the approximate value, where pi is used.
